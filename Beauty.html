3 Natural ways to get glowing skin

January 3, 2020

1. Honey

Honey when taken internally and externally is excellent for the skin. For combination to dry skin types, take one teaspoon of honey and massage on the skin daily. Leave it on for about 10 minutes and then wash. Pat dry and you will see that not only is your skin softer but also glowing. Make sure you use raw honey both internally and externally in order to reap maximum benefits. Organic raw honey is easily available everywhere nowadays.

2. Milk

I personally love this ingredient. Not only does it softens and nourishes the skin but also makes it sensuous and helps in de-tanning. Wipe your skin with full fat milk twice a day or just splash a small bowlful of cold milk on your skin and pat dry. Milk is known to be one of the best moisturisers for your skin. This one's a great home remedy for those who have a dry skin. Ensure that you apply it regularly.

3. Almond

Whether you are using pure almond oil or almond paste, both are excellent sources of Vitamin E, providing nourishment for your skin externally. For dehydrated and dry skin, first massage the face with one teaspoon almond oil and then make a paste of 10 almonds mixed with a little milk and scrub it gently onto the skin. Wash the face with milk and then water. Not only will this help patchy skin but will give the skin an youthful texture, something we are all in constant search of.

3 Famous Hairdos That Are Always In Style


January 30, 2020

1.The sleek ponytail

The 2018 met Gala saw the best version of Kim Kardashian West’s sleek high ponytail. A sleek ponytail suits all hair types. It can be done for a formal as well as informal looks. To do a sleek ponytail blowdry your hair, prop it with some dry oil. Now run your fingers through your scalp while spraying some hair setting spray. Now make your ponytail. After doing the ponytail take the strands of hair from the ponytail and straight iron it. You are sleek ponytail is done. This is the most popular hairstyle among celebrities and it’s quite easy to do. It is one of the oldest fashion trends that are still being followed and the are quite popular.

2.French Braid

Braiding is a unique style of hair that is chic as well as formal and even sporty. French braid is braiding three strands of hair from the crown of your head to the cape of your neck and then securing with a hair tie. It’s not always essential that a french braid is a single standing hairstyle. Sometimes it is used to make buns or different kind of hairstyles depending on what you want. Braiding is easy when your head is of a single length and not layered. French braid is not the easiest braiding but it sure is the best kind of all day hairstyle if you want your hair to be friz and tangle free.

3.Beachy Waves

The loose long curls or popularly known as beachy waves is a personal favourite of every celebrity to fashion lover. These curls are not limp but just soft, supple spirals of hair. To get this hairstyle is quite a difficult task. But there enough tutorials available for all your hair types, long or short, with and without hot ironing. This hairstyle is just perfect to nail any look you want from, the runway model to the carefree, I-dance-with-the-devil look. This hairstyle has been seen in ref carpets all around the world. This hairstyle suits all hair types thin or thick. The best part of this hairstyle is that it gives you a look of a natural hair do which is supple and carefree and at the same time kind of sophisticated.
